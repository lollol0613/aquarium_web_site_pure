  • We invite you to come see our world-famous Mermaids of Auckalnd Aquarium as they perform the version of Director Jack's The Beautiful Mermaid live in our submerged 400-seat auditorium. You will enjoy this classic told unlike any other as The Beautiful Mermaid celebrates her birthday and comes face to face with her Prince! Yearning to have legs like him, she makes a deal with the sea witch to give her what she wants in exchange for her beautiful voice.

mermaid show

The mermaid shows are included with the price of admission.

SHOWTIMES
The Little Mermaid: 11 AM & 3 PM (Saturday & Sunday) / 3 PM (Monday-Friday)
Fish Tails: 1:30 PM (Saturday & Sunday) / 11 AM (Monday-Friday)

THE MERMAIDS PERFORM DAILY, 365 DAYS A YEAR.

Shows are subject to change.
(Theater doors open 30 minutes prior to the show. Once the theater is at capacity, the doors will close. Seating is on a first-come basis. Shoes & shirts required to enter the theater.)
