feeding programme

During some animals feeding sessions some lucky children are invited to come in to the animals enclosure to feed a fish to the animals.

- Octopus : 10:30 and 16:30 (Feeds at 11:00)
- Sharks : 11:00, 14:30 and 16:00(Feeds at 14:30 on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays only)
- Penguins : 12:00 and 15:00(Feeds at 12:00 and 15:00 daily, except for Wednesdays at 12:00)
- Rays : 11:30 and 15:30(Feeds at 11:30)
- Jellyfish : 16:30 (Feeds on Wednesday only)
