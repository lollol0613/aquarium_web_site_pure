HOW TO
FIND
AUCKLAND AQUARIUM

AUCKLAND AUQARIUM is located on Marine Parade, Napier in sunny Hawke's Bay. The aquarium is a scenic 15 minute walk from Napier's city centre, or a brisk 5 minute cycle ride along the waterfront.

Address: Auckland Aquarium, Marine Parade, Napier, Auckland, New zealand

PARKING

  • Large car park including bus and camper parking
  • Disability parking (easy access for visitors with disabilities is also available throughout the aquarium)
