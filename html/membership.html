annual membership

Your Auckland Aquarium Annual Membership guarantees you and your family new thrills, adventures and fish-filled fun each time you visit. Members can take advantage of free and discounted admission to special events, discounts on Aquarium programs and more. Plus with the additional discounts, Memberships pay for themselves in just two visits. With new animals and activities planned throughout the year, you will have plenty of reasons to come back again and again.

* Memberships are valid for one year from the date of purchase.
